Built from the ground up

Chase Johnson started doing yard work at age 10. Deep Roots Nursery is the continuation of that hands-on path — not a corporate rollout.

Today, Chase and Madison Johnson operate a full-service nursery and landscape company serving Old River-Winfree, Mont Belvieu, Baytown, and the greater Houston area. The business has grown through real work — including acquiring Donath's Garden Works and Chase's Yard Care & Landscaping — so customers can buy plants and materials and hire the same team for installation and ongoing care.
